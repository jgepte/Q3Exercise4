Laguna

Laguna is a well-known province in the Philippines. It is the resort capital of the country, housing more than 700 hot spring resorts in its several cities. It's hot springs are what I think make it so beloved and often well visited, making it one of the best tourist resorts and vacation spots in the Philippines in my opinion. While not the most popular beach resort, it's beach resorts are visually stunning and delivers great service.
